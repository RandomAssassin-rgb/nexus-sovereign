# Business Model Notes

## Positioning
B2B2C embedded parametric income protection for gig workers.

## Core weekly model
- worker pays a weekly premium
- partner may subsidize a portion
- insurer receives controlled parametric exposure
- zero-touch payouts reduce servicing cost

## Metrics to show
- weekly premium
- payout cap
- expected claims frequency
- reserve usage
- loss ratio by trigger
- fraud-adjusted margin
